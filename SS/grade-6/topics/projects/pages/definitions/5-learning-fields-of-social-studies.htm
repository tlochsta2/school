Definitions

5 Learning Fields of Social Studies - Minecraft Edition Video

  • History: A RECORD OF THE PAST
  • Geography: THE STUDY OF PEOPLE, PLACE, AND THE ENVIRONMENT
  • Culture: THE BELIEFS, CUSTOMS, LAWS, ART, & WAYS OF LIVING THAT A GROUP OF PEOPLE SHARE
  • Government: THE PEOPLE & GROUPS WITHIN A SOCIETY THAT HAVE THE AUTHORITY TO MAKE LAWS, TO MAKE SURE THEY ARE CARRIED OUT, & TO SETTLE DISAGREEMENTS
  • Economics: THE STUDY OF HOW RESOURCES ARE MANAGED IN THE PRODUCTION, EXCHANGE, & USE OF GOODS & SERVICES
